Fraser Island. An amazing experience.

I booked on to a 3 day tour with a group of 20 people. We were split up into Jeeps and drove ourselves to the Island where we cooked and camped together, which was really sociable and really good fun. During the days we cruised the beach in the jeep, stopping off at shipwrecks and waterfalls.

Great Barrier Reef. Simply beautiful.

I am not a huge fan of swimming in the sea generally, however crystal clear water and fish of every colour changed my mind on sea swimming. I wasn't brave enough to scuba dive but saw loads just from snorkelling.
